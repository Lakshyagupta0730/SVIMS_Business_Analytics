About Us

The Business Analytic Club is a dynamic group of students passionate about data, business strategies, and analytical thinking. Our club aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world business applications by encouraging students to explore data-driven decision-making. Through workshops, projects, and collaborative learning, we help members develop essential skills in analytics, problem-solving, and innovation. Whether you are a beginner or have prior experience, our club provides a supportive platform to grow, learn, and excel in the field of business analytics.
